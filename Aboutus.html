About Rumah Anak Yatim & Asnaf Assolihin

وَيَسْأَلُونَكَ عَنِ الْيَتَامَى قُلْ إِصْلاَحٌ لَّهُمْ خَيْرٌ وَإِنْ تُخَالِطُوهُمْ فَإِخْوَانُكُمْ

(Surah Al-Baqarah, Ayat 220)

“And they ask you about orphans. Say, 'Improving their condition is best, and if you mix with them – they are your brothers.'”

Rumah Anak Yatim dan Asnaf Assolihin was established out of the compassion of a married couple towards underprivileged children. Based in Banting, Selangor, it provides shelter, education, and moral guidance to orphans and asnaf in need. With love and dedication, the home focuses on building a generation that is knowledgeable, respectful, and spiritually guided.
