VISION

To become a replacement home that nurtures a generation with noble character, high knowledge, and diverse skills in the fields they pursue.

MISION

1.To manage the welfare and assistance for orphans who are underprivileged and facing difficulties.


2.To provide opportunities for the residents of this orphanage to receive better education as a catalyst for a knowledgeable and skilled future workforce.


3. To provide special education especially in religious knowledge to ensure the generation raised by Rumah Anak Yatim dan Asnaf As-Solihin has good morals, while also providing proper shelter, food and ethical upbringing.


4.To uphold trust and responsibilities, especially in managing the properties of the residents and donations provided, in order to strengthen both physical development and personal identity of every child under this care.


5.To create a conducive environment for the residents to support their mental and emotional growth effectively and without disruption.

History of Establishment

Rumah Anak Yatim dan Asnaf As-Solihin was established in January 2010. Located in Banting, Selangor, at Lot 839, Jalan Aman, Kanchong Darat, 42700, Selangor, it originally served as a family home for a married couple—Ustaz Molyana and his wife. The idea of establishing this home came from their compassion towards less fortunate children and the desire to provide them a better and more secure future.
